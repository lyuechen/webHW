When someone does something good, applaud! You will make two people happy.

2019-05-31

When someone does something good, applaud! You will make two people happy. —— Samuel Goldwyn

当有人做了好事，就鼓掌！这样他/她和你自己都会开心。—— 塞缪尔·戈尔德温（美国电影制片人）

applaud[v.鼓掌]: When a group of people applaud, they clap their hands in order to show approval, for example, when they have enjoyed a play or concert.

All the audience stood to applaud his impressive performance. 所有观众起立为他令人印象深刻的表演鼓掌。

applaud[v.称赞，赞许]: When an attitude or action is applauded, people praise it.

She applauds the fact that they are creating new opportunities for others. 他们在为别人创造新机会，对此她表示赞赏。
